History

Cascade Rapids Adventures has been thrilling seekers of untamed waters since 1985. Nestled in the heart of the Evergreen Valley, our family-owned operation combines adrenaline-pumping rapids with unparalleled wilderness experiences. From the roaring Class IV Thunder Run to the beginner-friendly Osprey Route, our certified guides bring decades of river wisdom to every expedition.

We don't just offer rafting trips – we create stories that will echo in your memory like the sound of rushing water through ancient canyons. With state-of-the-art equipment, riverside gourmet lunches, and exclusive access to some of the most pristine stretches of the Wild River, Cascade Rapids Adventures turns ordinary travelers into river warriors. Join us for a day of adventure, or embark on our legendary five-day wilderness expedition. The river is calling.
